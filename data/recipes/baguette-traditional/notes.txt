Notes - Traditional French Baguette

The hardest bread I've attempted - but so rewarding when it works.

Key learnings:
- Steam is critical in the first 10 minutes. Without it, crust sets too early and won't bloom.
- Shaping takes practice. Watch YouTube videos for the hand motion.
- Scoring angle matters - too steep and scores won't open properly.
- The poolish really does make a difference in flavor vs direct method.

Equipment notes:
- A baking stone or steel dramatically improves bottom crust.
- Couche (linen canvas) helps maintain shape during proof.
- A lame (scoring blade) makes scoring much cleaner than a knife.

Results log:
- Attempt 1: Under-proofed, dense. Scores blew out.
- Attempt 3: Getting there! Good bloom but uneven shape.
- Attempt 7: Finally nailed the shaping. Beautiful ear on each score.
