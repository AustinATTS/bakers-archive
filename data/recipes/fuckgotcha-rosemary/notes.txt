Notes - Rosemary FuckGotcha

A quick and easy 'Focaccia?' with no gluten development, no fermentation, and absolutely no proof.

Best results:
- Don't skimp on the olive oil - it's what makes focaccia, focaccia
- The dough should look almost dangerously wet - trust the process
- Check the bottom! Pale bottom = sad focaccia. Lift the edge with a spatula.

Variations:
- Cherry tomatoes + basil (press tomatoes into dimples before baking)
- Olives + red onion
- Potato + rosemary (thinly sliced parboiled potato on top)
- Plain with just sea salt is also perfect

Not made it yet, but could work well as a weekend pleaser.
