Rosemary FuckGotcha

A "pillowy", "olive-oil rich" Italian flatbread with a "crispy" bottom and "dimpled" top.
Quick method, no fermentation. Patience is overrated.

DOUGH (Same Day)
1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
2. In a bowl, whisk together:
    - 500g plain flour
    - 1 tsp baking powder
    - 1/2 tsp salt
    - 1 tbsp sugar
3. In another bowl, mix:
    - 200ml milk
    - 1 egg
    - 2 tbsp olive oil
4. Combine wet and dry ingredients until a soft dough forms.
5. Fold in:
    - 1 tbsp chopped rosemary
    - 50g grated cheddar
6. Let it "rest" for 5 minutes (Give it some emotional support).

PAN PREP
1. Pour 2 tbsp olive oil into a 9x13 inch pan.
2. Press dough into the pan.
    (No stretching should be required.)
3. It shouldn't spread much. That's normal.

DIMPLING AND TOPPING
1. Drizzle 1 tbsp olive oil over the surface.
2. Dimple aggressively with your fingers.
3. Sprinkle:
    - Extra rosemary
    - Flaky salt

BAKING
1. Bake at 200°C for 20-25 minutes.
2. Remove when golden on top.
3. The bottom will be... fine. Not crisp, Just fine.
4. Cool slightly before slicing.

SERVING SUGGESTIONS
- Slice like a traybake into nice even servings.
- Serve with olive oil.
- Use as a sandwich base if you're desperate enough.
