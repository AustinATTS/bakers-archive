Whole Wheat Sandwich Bread

OVERVIEW
A soft, tender whole wheat sandwich loaf. Uses enrichments (honey, butter, milk) 
for a softer crumb and longer shelf life.

INGREDIENTS
- 360g whole wheat flour
- 120g bread flour
- 280ml warm water (105-110°F)
- 2 tsp instant yeast
- 1.5 tsp salt
- 2 tbsp honey
- 2 tbsp unsalted butter, softened
- 1/4 cup dry powdered milk

INSTRUCTIONS

MIXING
1. Combine warm water, honey, and yeast. Let bloom 5 minutes.
2. Add whole wheat flour, bread flour, powdered milk, and salt.
3. Mix until shaggy dough forms.
4. Add softened butter and knead 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
5. Dough should pass windowpane test.

FIRST RISE
1. Place dough in greased bowl, cover.
2. Rise at room temperature 1-1.5 hours until doubled.

SHAPING
1. Punch down dough, turn onto work surface.
2. Flatten into rectangle about 8 inches wide.
3. Roll tightly into log, pinch seam to seal.
4. Place seam-down in greased 9x5 loaf pan.

SECOND RISE
1. Cover loosely and let rise 45-60 minutes.
2. Dough should crown about 1 inch above pan rim.

BAKING
1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. Bake 30-35 minutes until golden brown.
3. Internal temperature should reach 190-200°F.
4. Cool completely before slicing (at least 1 hour).
