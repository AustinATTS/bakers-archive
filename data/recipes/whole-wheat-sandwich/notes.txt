Notes - Whole Wheat Sandwich Bread

This is my go-to everyday bread. Kids love it!

Variations tried:
- Added 2 tbsp ground flaxseed - great nutrition boost
- Replaced honey with molasses - darker color, richer flavor
- Added 1/4 cup rolled oats on top for texture

Troubleshooting:
- If loaf is dense: make sure yeast is fresh and water is the right temp
- If top cracks: dough may need more water, or rose too fast
- Freeze extras sliced - thaws perfectly in toaster
