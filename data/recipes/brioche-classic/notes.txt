Notes - Classic Brioche

Rich, indulgent, and worth every gram of butter.

Critical tips:
- Butter temperature matters! Too cold = dough breaks. Too warm = greasy mess. 
  Aim for butter that dents easily but isn't shiny/oily.
- Overnight cold proof is non-negotiable for flavor.
- The butter incorporation phase feels like it's going wrong - keep going, it comes together.

Uses for leftover/stale brioche:
- Best French toast you'll ever make
- Bread pudding
- Pain perdu with orange custard

Variations:
- Add orange zest and a splash of Grand Marnier
- Chocolate chips (add in last 2 min of butter phase)
- Make into buns for burgers - transforms any burger

The dough freezes well after shaping - proof from frozen overnight in fridge, 
then room temp 4-6 hours before baking.
