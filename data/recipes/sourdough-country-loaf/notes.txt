Personal Notes - Sourdough Country Loaf

First attempt: Good oven spring but crumb was a bit tight. Starter may not have been at peak.

Second attempt: Used starter at peak (doubled and domed) - much better open crumb!

Tips:
- Bulk time varies a lot with temperature. Use the dough, not the clock.
- The cold retard (up to 18 hrs) really develops flavor.
- Scoring angle matters - steeper scores bloom more.
- My oven runs hot, so I reduce to 430°F for the uncovered bake.
