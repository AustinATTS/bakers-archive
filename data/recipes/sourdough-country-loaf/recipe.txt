Sourdough Country Loaf

OVERVIEW
This classic country sourdough uses a high-hydration dough for an open crumb and crispy crust.

LEVAIN (Night Before)
- Mix 1 tbsp mature starter with 50g flour and 50g water
- Let ripen 8-12 hours at room temperature until bubbly

AUTOLYSE
1. Combine 450g bread flour, 50g whole wheat flour with 350g water (hold 25g)
2. Mix until no dry flour remains
3. Rest 30-45 minutes

MIXING
1. Add 100g ripe levain and mix to incorporate
2. Add 10g salt dissolved in remaining 25g water
3. Squeeze through fingers until fully mixed

BULK FERMENTATION (4-5 hours at 75-78°F)
- Perform 4 sets of stretch and folds during first 2 hours
- Each set: 4 folds with 30-minute rest between sets
- Dough should increase 50-75% in volume

SHAPING
1. Turn onto unfloured surface, pre-shape into round
2. Rest 20-30 minutes
3. Final shape: use bench knife to tighten into batard or boule

PROOFING
- Place in floured banneton seam-side up
- Refrigerate 12-16 hours (cold retard)

BAKING
1. Preheat Dutch oven at 500°F for 1 hour
2. Score dough with lame at 45-degree angle
3. Bake covered 20 minutes
4. Remove lid, reduce to 450°F, bake 20-25 minutes until deep mahogany
5. Cool on wire rack at least 1 hour before cutting
